// 01 — About

Systems Thinker.
Hardware Builder.

I'm a PCB design and embedded systems engineer. When I look at a design, I'm thinking about the whole picture: where it might fail, how it gets built, and what happens when the requirements shift.

That kind of thinking shows up the same way whether I'm laying out a board, writing firmware, or planning system architecture.

My strongest work is at the hardware/software boundary: where silicon constraints, peripheral behaviour, power budgets, and software all have to work together.
